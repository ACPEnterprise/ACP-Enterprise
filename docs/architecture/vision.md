# Platform Vision

## Mission

Build the operating system that All County Plumbing & Leak uses to acquire customers, schedule and dispatch work, equip technicians, close sales, complete jobs, collect payment, and understand business performance—without Housecall Pro.

Version 1.0 succeeds only when the company can run its daily service operation reliably in ACP Enterprise and retire Housecall Pro for those workflows.

## Product vision

ACP Enterprise will provide one coherent operational record from the first customer contact through completed work and collected cash. Office staff, dispatchers, technicians, managers, and owners should see the same facts, appropriate to their roles, without re-entering data or reconciling conflicting systems.

The product should make the correct next action obvious, preserve a trustworthy history of business activity, and expose timely operational intelligence. It is an operating platform, not merely a collection of forms or a reporting dashboard.

## Business goals

For version 1.0:

- Replace Housecall Pro for All County Plumbing & Leak without disrupting customer service or revenue collection.
- Reduce duplicate entry across calls, customers, jobs, estimates, schedules, invoices, and payments.
- Give dispatch and management a real-time view of workload, technician status, revenue, and exceptions.
- Standardize operational workflows while retaining explicit overrides with an audit trail.
- Own and preserve the company's operational data in a model that supports future products and integrations.

For subsequent versions:

- Replace QuickBooks where ACP Enterprise can provide complete accounting controls and reporting.
- Improve margins through dispatch optimization, conversion intelligence, capacity planning, and automation.
- Package proven operating capabilities as a secure multi-company SaaS platform.

## Long-term strategy

ACP Enterprise will evolve in deliberate layers:

1. **Operational replacement:** establish a reliable system of record for the existing company's Housecall Pro workflows.
2. **Revenue and financial depth:** connect pricebook, estimates, invoices, payments, and operational reporting; later introduce accounting capabilities sufficient to replace QuickBooks.
3. **Intelligent operations:** use trustworthy event history and domain data for forecasting, anomaly detection, recommendations, and workflow automation.
4. **SaaS platform:** generalize tenant isolation, configuration, onboarding, billing, support, and compliance for multiple home-service companies.

Capabilities required only for later layers should be anticipated through clean boundaries, but they must not be prematurely built into version 1.0.

## Competitive position

Housecall Pro and similar field-service products optimize for a broad market and standardized workflows. ACP Enterprise begins with a narrower advantage: it can encode the exact operating model of All County Plumbing & Leak, integrate its workflows end to end, and evolve from direct operational feedback.

The long-term differentiation is not feature count. It is a trusted operational data foundation combined with faster workflow execution, deeper business intelligence, and automation that understands the complete customer and job lifecycle.

## North Star

**Every customer commitment, operational action, and dollar of revenue is visible, trustworthy, and actionable in ACP Enterprise.**

The primary version 1.0 success measure is operational independence: All County Plumbing & Leak completes normal daily workflows in ACP Enterprise with no Housecall Pro dependency and no material loss of service, data, or financial control.

Supporting indicators include:

- Percentage of eligible jobs managed entirely in ACP Enterprise
- Schedule and dispatch reliability
- Time from inbound request to booked appointment
- Estimate approval and job completion rates
- Time from job completion to payment
- Data reconciliation exceptions and manual workarounds
- Platform availability and workflow error rate during business hours
